About Elizabeth

Elizabeth Kelley is a rising figure known for her dedication to serving the greater good. She has achieved more in her teenage years than most would in a lifetime and continues to pursue her passion for learning in all facets of life. With a passion and recognized dedication to women's health, trauma education, childcare, culture, the outdoors, and music, she is recognized for her dedication and hard work.

Accomplishments & Aspirations

  • Public Speaker: Prepares talks to anyone, anywhere, focusing on inspiring change and innovation, and finding purpose.
  • Future Author: Currently working on her first book exploring themes of growing up, resilience, and bravery through battles. She is yet to announce 2025 publish date.
  • Founder of charity: In the process of starting a charity focused on supporting women, men, and children dealing with adversity.
  • Documentary Series Creator: Developing a documentary series where she immerses herself in different jobs every two weeks.
